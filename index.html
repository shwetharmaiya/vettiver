Our history

Vettiver Farmers is to showcase vettiver based products through the online medium.

We have been in this business for quite a long time hoping to leave a legacy in the customers' hands. We offer a range of products from Yoga mats to Vettiver handbag made from vettiver.

Vetiiver or vetiver is also called khus-khus. It is native to tropical Asia. Its scientific name is Chrysopogon zizanioides. Its root is used for perfumes. Vetiver is sometimes referred to as the “Oil of Tranquility” or “The Fragrance of the Soil” for its calming effects.
